Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: July 20, 2026

If you would like Yash and Co Ventures to delete your personal data collected through our WhatsApp business messaging service, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email the published business contact email below.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include the WhatsApp number you used to contact Yash and Co Ventures and sufficient details to locate your records.
  4. We may need to verify your identity or ownership of the phone number before proceeding.
  5. Eligible records will be deleted or anonymised within a reasonable period.
  6. Records that must be retained for legal, fraud-prevention, accounting or contractual reasons may not be deleted immediately.
  7. We will confirm completion of your request through the contact email or WhatsApp number you used to make the request.
